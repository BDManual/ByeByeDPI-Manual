All releases of the application are hosted on GitHub:  
**[github.com/romanvht/ByeByeDPI/releases](https://github.com/romanvht/ByeByeDPI/releases)**

## How to Download

### Step 1: Go to the Releases Page

Open the [releases page](https://github.com/romanvht/ByeByeDPI/releases) on GitHub.

### Step 2: Select the Needed Release

Find the version you want and expand **Assets** at the bottom of the release.

### Step 3: Choose APK for Your Device

In the Assets section, you'll find several APK files:

- **arm64-v8a** - for most modern Android devices (recommended)
- **armeabi-v7a** - for older Android devices
- **x86** and **x86_64** - for emulators and specialized devices
- **universal** - universal version (works on all devices, but larger file size)

#### How to Find Out Your Device's Architecture?

If you don't know your device's architecture:

1. Open **Settings** > **About phone**
2. Find **System information**
3. Look for architecture information (usually **arm64** or **ARM64**)

**If unsure** - download the **universal** version. It works on all devices.

### Step 4: Install APK

After downloading the APK file:

1. Open the file on your Android device
2. Allow installation from unknown sources (if prompted)
3. Tap **Install**

Done! ByeByeDPI is now installed on your device.
